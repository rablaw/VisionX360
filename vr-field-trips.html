VR Field Trips for Schools

VisionX360 offers immersive VR field trips for schools, allowing students to explore educational environments and historical experiences without leaving the classroom.

What Are VR Field Trips?

VR field trips use virtual reality headsets to take students into realistic 360-degree learning experiences. Instead of watching a normal video, students feel as if they are inside the place, event, or story.

Why Schools Use VR Field Trips

Schools use VR field trips to make learning more engaging, visual, and memorable. This type of activity is especially useful for subjects such as history, geography, culture, science, religion, and social studies.

Classroom-Based Experience

The activity can be delivered inside the school itself. Students do not need transportation, outdoor coordination, or a physical trip. VisionX360 brings the VR equipment and manages the experience for groups of students during the school day.

Educational Value

A VR field trip helps students connect emotionally and visually with the topic. It can support classroom lessons, school events, cultural days, and educational enrichment programs.

Suitable for School Groups

VisionX360 experiences are designed for organized school groups. The system allows many students to participate throughout the day in scheduled sessions.
